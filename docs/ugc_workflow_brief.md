- To start, I need exactly: (1) a micro-portfolio of 3–5 sample videos of products I already own, shot on my phone; (2) a clear niche; (3) platform accounts.
- Recommended beginner platform stack: JoinBrands (marketplace, brands post briefs, creators apply, $100+ per project), Billo (brand-side briefs, fast turnaround, vetted creator pool), UGCJobs.com (job board). Bounty is different — it's TikTok-based (post about products on your own TikTok, paid per views in the first 48h), so it DOES depend on posting publicly and getting views; treat it as optional, not the core path.
